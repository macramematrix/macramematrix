Our Story

About Macrame Matrix

Hi, I'm Monika — the creator behind Macrame Matrix. After spending more than 15 years in the corporate world, I took a break and decided to explore macrame as a way to keep myself engaged. That curiosity quickly turned into something I truly enjoy — a deep love for knots, patterns, and designs that bring warmth and charm to everyday spaces.

For me, macrame is more than just art — it feels like therapy. It has helped me heal, reconnect with myself, and find a sense of peace in creating something meaningful with my own hands. Based in Pune, India, I channel that passion into every piece, blending traditional knotting techniques with modern design sensibilities.

Each piece I create is designed with simplicity and attention to detail, so it can beautifully fit into your home or personal space. Our tagline "Knotted with Precision" isn't just a phrase — it's a promise that every loop, every knot, and every tassel reflects hours of careful artistry and an unwavering dedication to quality.

Macrame Matrix is not just about decor — it's about creating something meaningful, one knot at a time. When you own a piece from us, you're welcoming a handcrafted story into your space. Thank you for being here and supporting this journey.

Eco-Conscious Craft

Our Sustainable Approach

At Macrame Matrix, sustainability is woven into every fiber. We're committed to creating beauty that respects both people and the planet.

Eco-Friendly Materials

We source organic cotton, recycled cord, biodegradable fibers, jute, and hemp. Every material is chosen for its environmental integrity and durability, ensuring your piece lasts for years to come.

Minimal Waste Practices

We carefully plan every project to minimize scraps and repurpose leftover materials. Our studio operates with a zero-waste mindset, transforming potential waste into new creative possibilities.

Local & Ethical Sourcing

We prioritize local suppliers and ethical sourcing practices. By sourcing our materials from trusted local vendors in and around Pune, we reduce our carbon footprint while supporting the community we call home.

Our Mission

Handcrafted Quality, Accessible to All

At Macrame Matrix, our mission is to craft high-quality macrame products at prices that make handmade art accessible to every home. Created by the skilled hands of artisan craftspeople in Pune, India, our products are designed to add beauty and functionality to your living spaces — from essential home and pet accessories to stunning wall hangings and personal-use accessories.

Every piece we create is more than just decor — it's a blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern style, thoughtfully made to enrich your everyday life.

Our Vision

Making Macrame a Global Art Form

Our vision is to grow Macrame Matrix into a globally recognized brand for macrame artistry. We are passionate about making this exquisite form of traditional craft accessible to every home and keeping it affordable without compromising on quality.

Beyond beautiful products, we aspire to create meaningful work opportunities and financial support for artisans and aspiring craftspeople. We believe macrame can empower communities — one knot, one opportunity at a time.
