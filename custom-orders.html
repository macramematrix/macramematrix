The Process

How We Bring Your Ideas to Life

01

Consultation

Share your vision with us. Tell us about your style, the space where it will live, color preferences, size, and any special inspiration. We'll discuss possibilities, answer your questions, and understand what makes your dream piece special.

02

Design

We sketch out your custom design, taking your feedback into account. You'll see visual concepts of how your piece will look. Once you approve the design, we finalize all the details including knot patterns, materials, and finishing touches.

03

Creation

Our hands get to work. Each knot is tied with precision using sustainably sourced, eco-friendly materials. We handcraft your piece with the same care and attention we put into every piece of Macrame Matrix art. You'll receive progress updates along the way.

04

Delivery

Your finished custom piece is carefully packaged and delivered to you. We'll include care instructions to help your macrame stay beautiful for years. We love seeing how our pieces fit into your space—share photos with us!
